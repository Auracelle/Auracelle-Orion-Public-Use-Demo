Auracelle Orion v4
CYBER GOVERNANCE WARGAMING SIMULATION · AURACELLE AI GOVERNANCE LABS · 2026

Auracelle Orion v4 is a turn-based cyber governance wargaming simulation that stress-tests policy decisions across realistic supply chain attack scenarios. Each 5-turn scenario compresses a full incident lifecycle — from initial SCTL entry through governance consolidation — into a structured decision environment that quantifies the quality of your governance choices using the E-AGPO-HT v2.0 framework.

E-AGPO-HT v2.0 — ANALYTICAL FRAMEWORK

The simulation is powered by the E-AGPO-HT v2.0 framework (Evans-Accelerated Governance Policy Optimization - Hierarchical Theory), a proprietary governance intelligence architecture. The g-GCC master score integrates governance performance signals across multiple institutional dimensions. Decision quality is assessed by Ψ_v2 — a composite governance index calibrated to real-world policy frameworks.

HOW TO PLAY
① Spend intel tokens to reveal hidden info  ② Choose from 4 governance options  ③ Review E-AGPO-HT consequences  ④ Aim Ψ_v2 ≥ 0.50 · Load your own governance artifacts to personalise the baseline
